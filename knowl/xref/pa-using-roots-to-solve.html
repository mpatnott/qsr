Prep Activity 12.1.3.

In this unit, we will need to be able to solve equations of the form
\begin{equation*} y = cx^n\text{,} \end{equation*}
where the values of \(y\text{,}\) \(c\text{,}\) and \(n\) are known.
🔗
  1. Divide by sides by \(c\text{.}\)
    🔗
    🔗
  2. When \(n\) is a positive whole number, take the n-th root of both sides.
    🔗
    🔗
